My Admission into SP
I was admitted to Singapore polytechnic after my N' Levels in 2019. Back then, I was under the Polytechnic Foundation Programme, where N' Level Students were given a chance to go to Polytechnic straight away after N' Levels to complete a foundation year.

Polytechnic Foundation Programme

My PFP Experience
In PFP, we were all exposed to the Poly' style environment for learning. It was a life changer for me as I was used to getting spoon-fed by my secondary school teachers to learn things. When I came into poly, I couldn't get used to the 'Self-Directed' learning method; I had a hard time adjusting my old ways.
As time went by, PFP has taught me to be more self-initiative, and take more responsibility for my own learning.
Click here to be redirected to SP's PFP Website.

Post PFP
Now that I am in year 1, PFP has allowed me to quickly adjust myself to the pace of the Polytechnic style of learning. Not only that, I was also able to share with my fellow peers about my experience and share them my skills I have learnt during PFP.
